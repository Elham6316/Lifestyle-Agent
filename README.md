# 🌴 Lifestyle Agent

**Your AI entertainment buddy — not a chatbot, a friend with good taste.**

Lifestyle Agent is a personalized AI assistant built on Claude Projects that helps users make the most of their free time. Instead of generic suggestions, it learns your taste from a personal profile and recommends movies, shows, documentaries, games, books, podcasts, and weekend plans that actually fit your mood, budget, and energy level — then explains *why* each pick works for you.

---

## ✨ Why It's Different

Most recommendation bots ask a question and spit out a list. Lifestyle Agent:

- **Remembers your taste** — reads a personal entertainment profile (genres, franchises, dislikes, streaming services, budget) before recommending anything
- **Reads the room** — asks about your time, energy, and mood first, and only when needed
- **Thinks beyond movies** — if a sunset walk with a podcast fits your mood better than a film, it'll say so
- **Explains itself** — every recommendation comes with a "why this fits you" reason, not just a title
- **Has a personality** — talks like a friend who knows your taste, not customer support

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. **Understand the moment** — Lifestyle Agent asks lightweight questions when it needs to:
   - How much free time do you have?
   - Alone, or with friends/family?
   - Staying in or heading out?
   - What's the mood — exciting, relaxing, funny, adventurous, inspiring?

2. **Personalize** — it cross-references your answers with your saved entertainment profile (favorite genres, franchises, streaming services, dislikes, budget) to filter out anything that's not a match.

3. **Recommend with reasoning** — results are delivered in clear tables (title, genre, runtime, mood, why-you'll-like-it) with a fun, energetic tone — never robotic.

4. **Close strong** — every session ends with a **⭐ My Top Pick For You Today**, the single best recommendation and the reasoning behind it.
